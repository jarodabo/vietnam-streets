Three Generations, One Heritage

The Nguyen Family • Silicon Valley, California

From boat refugees to tech entrepreneurs, the Nguyen family's journey spans three generations and embodies the Vietnamese-American dream. Their story illuminates how cultural identity evolves while remaining deeply rooted in tradition.

First Generation

Escaped Vietnam by boat in 1979, rebuilt life as restaurateurs in San Jose's Vietnamese district.

Second Generation

Bridged cultures, pursued higher education, maintained language and traditions while building professional careers.

Third Generation

Tech innovators reconnecting with heritage, teaching Vietnamese to their children, traveling back to Vietnam.
